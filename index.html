A New Kind of Honey

ReHive is a luxury creamed honey infused with real fruit and crafted in small batches.

Each jar contains locally sourced raw honey from a single Nottinghamshire apiary combined with vibrant fruit flavours created from surplus and foraged fruit.

The honey is carefully whipped to create a smooth spreadable texture and finished with a two-colour fruit swirl.

Every 227g jar of Raspberry Ripple contains the flavour of roughly 20 whole raspberries captured through our gentle drying and extraction process.
