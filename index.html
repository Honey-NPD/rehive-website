A New Expression of Honey

ReHive is a refined creamed honey infused with real fruit, crafted in small batches with precision and care.

Each jar begins with locally sourced raw honey from a single Nottinghamshire apiary, paired with vibrant fruit derived from surplus and foraged ingredients.

Through a controlled creaming process, the honey develops a smooth, spreadable texture, finished with a distinctive dual-colour fruit swirl.
