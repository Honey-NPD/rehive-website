A New Kind of Honey

ReHive is a luxury creamed honey infused with real fruit and crafted in small batches.

Each jar contains locally sourced raw honey from a single Nottinghamshire apiary combined with vibrant fruit flavours created from surplus and foraged fruit.

The honey is whipped to a smooth, spreadable texture, finished with a two-colour fruit swirl that gives each flavour its distinctive look.

Every 227g jar of Raspberry Ripple contains the flavour of ~20 whole raspberries captured through gentle drying and extraction.
