A New Kind of Honey

ReHive is a luxury creamed honey infused with real fruit and crafted in small batches.

Each jar contains locally sourced raw honey from a single Nottinghamshire apiary combined with vibrant fruit flavours created from surplus and foraged fruit.

The honey is whipped to create a smooth, spreadable texture before being finished with a distinctive two-colour fruit swirl.

Every 227g jar of Raspberry Ripple captures the flavour of roughly twenty whole raspberries using our gentle drying and extraction process.
